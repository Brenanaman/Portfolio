Infographic Book

Program » Adobe InDesign // Adobe Illustrator

Format » 36 7.5in x 7.5in

I had to create an infographic book about a country of my choosing for this project. I choose Germany because I hope to visit there eventually and since I've been learning the language. In order to accomplish this, I researched German culture and discovered that it is highly organized and effective. With straightforward infographics that guide the reader through the book in an elegant manner, I attempted to imitate this in my design. In order to inspire a sense of precision and order, which are essential to the nation's identity, I also used aspects of German architecture and color schemes. In order to make the book both interesting and simple to use, the design attempts to strike a balance between aesthetic appeal and informative clarity.
