From Forge to Table

Program » Adobe Illustrator

Format » 18in x 24in

For this project, I was tasked with designing an infographic poster that visually communicated the development of a product, so I chose to focus on the production of dice, inspired by my love of board games that incorporate them. Rather than presenting the information in a straightforward, instructional manner, I wanted the design to feel interactive and playful—much like the experience of rolling dice in a game. To achieve this, I structured my poster to resemble a board game, leading the viewer through the dice-making process as if they were progressing through different spaces on a game board, with distinct sections representing key stages such as material selection, molding, polishing, and numbering. I used bright visuals, game-inspired typography, and playful iconography to enhance the theme, making the process feel immersive rather than purely educational.
