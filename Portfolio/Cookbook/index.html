Typographical Cookbook

Program » Adobe InDesign

Format » Cookbook 8in x 10in

For this project, we were tasked with creating a cookbook that focused on both a theme and typography, so I decided to center mine around the vibrant energy of city nightlife. The concept of late-night cravings and the excitement of finding food after hours seemed perfect for exploring through design. I wanted the typography to reflect the dynamic and unpredictable vibe of the night, using bold, expressive typefaces while ensuring readability for the cookbook’s structure. I called it Midnight Munchies to capture the essence of spontaneous, adventurous eating in the city.
